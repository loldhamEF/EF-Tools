What Are Executive Functions?

Executive Functions are the brain-based skills required for humans to execute, or perform, tasks. They are the essential self-regulating skills that enable us to plan, organize, manage time, and persist to complete goals.

For students, strengthening these skills is the key to unlocking academic potential and building lifelong habits for success.
