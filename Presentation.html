How to View Student Data

1. Go to the Firebase Console

Navigate to console.firebase.google.com and log in.

2. Select Your Project

Click on your project from the list (e.g., `ef-in-action`).

3. Navigate to Firestore

In the left menu, under "Build," click on **Firestore Database**.

4. View the Collections

Click on `student_assessments` or `student_goals` to see the submitted data.
