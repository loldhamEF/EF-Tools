My Executive Functioning Skills Check-In

This is a tool to help you discover your strengths and plan for growth this year!

This is not a test! There are no right or wrong answers. The goal is to help you understand your own skills. Please read each statement and choose the number that best describes you. Your honest answers will help you and your teachers set meaningful goals.

Section A: Materials Management

1. I use a binder and/or folders to keep my school materials organized.

2. I can find what I need in my backpack and locker without much trouble.

3. I have a system for keeping track of graded papers and old assignments.

Section B: Time Management & Planning

4. I use a planner to write down my homework and assignments.

5. I can estimate how long it will take me to complete my homework.

6. I break down large projects into smaller, more manageable steps.

7. I plan my time for homework and other activities after school.

Section C: Study Strategies

8. I know how to identify the key words in essay questions to understand what the teacher is asking.

9. I use different methods of taking notes to help me remember what I'm learning.

10. I have a quiet place to study at home, free from distractions.

11. I create a study plan to prepare for tests.

12. After a test, I review my mistakes to understand what I can do better next time.

Section D: Goal Setting

13. I set academic goals for myself.

14. I can identify the steps I need to take to reach my goals.

15. I track my grades to see my progress in each class.

Section E: Self-Advocacy & Mindset

16. When I'm confused in class, I ask the teacher for help.

17. I know what accommodations I need and how to ask for them.

18. I believe that I can improve my skills with effort and practice.

This is a self-contained HTML file. You can send it directly to students via email or post it on your class website.

  • To Complete: Students open the file in any web browser (Chrome, Safari, etc.) and fill out the form directly on their device.
